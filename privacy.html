Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are paramount. AttendSync operates entirely locally on your device.

Data Processing

AttendSync is a client-side application. We do not store, upload, or transmit your WhatsApp chat logs, attendance data, or student records to any external servers. All text parsing, data analysis, and result generation happen entirely within your local browser environment.

Local Storage

Certain preferences, such as your theme selection (Dark/Light mode) and recent local states, might be saved using your browser's local storage capabilities (e.g., localStorage or sessionStorage). This is done purely to enhance your user experience and ensure your workflow is retained across page reloads. This local data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser cache.

Contact

If you have concerns about privacy, data security, or local processing within AttendSync, you can review the open-source code or reach out to the project maintainers directly.
